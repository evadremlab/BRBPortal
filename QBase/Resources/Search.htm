How To Search Using Query By Example

1) Type what you’re searching for in the field you want to search.

If your field has a dropdown, you can choose a value from the dropdown, or type in your own.

2) You can use the following symbols to make your search smarter:

Symbol

Meaning

Example(s)

>

Greater Than

>10 means 10 and anything higher than that

>D means starting with D or any letter higher than that.

>10/15/05 means October 15, 2005 and any date after that.
